# Team Profile

## Data Engineering Team
- 6 data engineers (3 senior, 2 mid, 1 junior)
- Strong skills: Python, SQL, dbt, Airflow, Snowflake
- Moderate skills: Kafka, Spark, Kubernetes
- Limited skills: Flink, real-time streaming, event sourcing

## Analytics Engineering Team
- 4 analytics engineers
- Strong skills: SQL, dbt, Looker, data modeling
- Focus on business logic and metric definitions

## Platform / Infrastructure
- 2 platform engineers (shared with other teams)
- Strong skills: Terraform, Kubernetes, AWS, CI/CD
- Limited bandwidth for data-specific infrastructure work

## Data Science
- 3 data scientists
- Strong skills: Python, scikit-learn, pandas, Jupyter
- Growing interest in MLOps and feature stores

## Organization
- All teams report to VP of Data
- Agile/Scrum methodology, 2-week sprints
- On-call rotation shared among senior data engineers
