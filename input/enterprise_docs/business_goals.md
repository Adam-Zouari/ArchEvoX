# Business Goals & Strategic Priorities

## Mission
Enable data-driven decision making across all departments with sub-hour data freshness for critical metrics.

## Key Business Goals

### 1. Real-Time Customer 360
- Build a unified customer view combining behavioral, transactional, and support data
- Target: < 5 minute latency for customer profile updates
- KPI: Customer lifetime value prediction accuracy > 85%

### 2. Revenue Operations Optimization
- Automated pipeline from lead scoring to revenue attribution
- Target: Daily revenue reporting available by 6 AM EST
- KPI: Sales cycle time reduction by 20%

### 3. Product Analytics at Scale
- Event-level product analytics for 10M+ monthly active users
- Target: Ability to query any product event within 15 minutes of occurrence
- KPI: Feature adoption measurement for 100% of new features

### 4. ML/AI Platform Foundation
- Provide clean, feature-ready data for machine learning models
- Target: Feature store with < 1 hour freshness
- KPI: Model training data pipeline completion < 4 hours

### 5. Data Governance & Compliance
- GDPR/CCPA compliance with automated PII detection and handling
- Target: Data lineage for 100% of PII fields
- KPI: Audit response time < 24 hours
